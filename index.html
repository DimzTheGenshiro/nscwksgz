About me

I'm a professional in web development and UI/UX design with over 5 years of experience. My expertise includes creating responsive websites, intuitive interface design, and developing efficient and user-friendly web applications. I'm committed to delivering digital solutions that are not only visually appealing but also functional and tailored to clients' needs.

My primary skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind CSS, Figma, and simple backend development using Node.js. I'm also comfortable working in a team and communicating with clients to ensure the final result meets their expectations.
